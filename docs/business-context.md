# Business Context

IronRidge field teams often see risk before formal office systems do. When issue tracking depends on email chains, informal notes, or personal spreadsheets, leadership loses time, support teams lose context, and closeout friction stays harder to manage than it should be.

## Current Realities

- issue visibility depends heavily on who is carrying the current follow-up
- severity and aging are not always visible in one shared view
- punch and closeout dependencies can remain disconnected from field issue tracking
- vendor and access issues can keep work open longer than office systems reflect

## Why Teams Care

Field-aware visibility helps both the office and the field understand what is open right now, who owns the next move, which issues are severe, and where closeout readiness is slipping.
